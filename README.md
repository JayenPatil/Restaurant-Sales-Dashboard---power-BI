# 🍽 Restaurant Sales & Performance Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Restaurant Sales & Performance Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI solution developed to analyze restaurant operations and business performance. It enables stakeholders to monitor sales, customer orders, restaurant performance, member activity, and meal insights through dynamic visualizations and KPIs.

This dashboard helps identify business trends, top-performing restaurants, customer behavior, and sales opportunities to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze overall restaurant sales performance
- Track customer order trends
- Monitor restaurant-wise performance
- Evaluate member purchasing behavior
- Analyze meal sales and pricing
- Provide interactive business insights through dashboards
